<p><p>The rare-earth bonded magnet market primarily includes two types: NdFeB (Neodymium-Iron-Boron) magnets and SmCo (Samarium-Cobalt) magnets. NdFeB magnets are known for their high magnetic strength and are widely used in applications like electric motors and hard drives. In contrast, SmCo magnets offer superior thermal stability and corrosion resistance, making them suitable for high-temperature environments and specialized applications. Both types are essential in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and consumer electronics, driven by advancements in technology and increasing demand for efficient magnetic solutions.</p></p>
