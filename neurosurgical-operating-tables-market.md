<p><ul><li>Electric Operating Tables</li><li>Hydraulic Operating Tables</li><li>Manual Operating Tables</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The neurosurgical operating tables market includes three main types: electric, hydraulic, and manual operating tables. Electric operating tables offer precise height and positioning adjustments through motorized controls, enhancing surgical efficiency. Hydraulic operating tables utilize fluid pressure for adjustments, providing stability and ease of movement, ideal for neurosurgical procedures. Manual operating tables rely on mechanical systems for positioning, requiring physical effort from the surgical team. Each type is essential for accommodating various surgical procedures and ensuring patient safety and comfort.</p></p>
