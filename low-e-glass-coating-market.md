<p><strong>The Low-E Glass Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sputtered (Soft Coating)</li><li>Pyrolytic (Hard Coating)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Low-E Glass Coating market comprises two main types: sputtered (soft coating) and pyrolytic (hard coating). Sputtered coatings are produced through a vacuum deposition process, offering high thermal performance and superior clarity, making them ideal for high-efficiency windows. Pyrolytic coatings, on the other hand, are applied during the glass manufacturing process and bond more rigidly, providing durability and resistance to scratches. Both types enhance energy efficiency by reflecting heat while allowing natural light to enter, catering to diverse architectural needs.</p></p>
