<p><p>A gelling agent is a substance used to thicken or solidify liquids, creating a gel-like consistency. They enhance texture, stability, and shelf life in products like jellies, desserts, creams, and medicines.</p><p>The Gelling Agent Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.8% during the forecast period. Consumers are seeking natural and clean-label ingredients, prompting manufacturers to innovate with plant-based and organic gelling agents. Additionally, the growing popularity of vegan and vegetarian diets has boosted the demand for alternatives to animal-derived gelling agents like gelatin.</p><p>Emerging trends include the development of multifunctional gelling agents that offer additional benefits such as improved nutritional profiles or enhanced sensory properties. The Asia-Pacific region is anticipated to witness significant growth due to rising disposable incomes, urbanization, and expanding food processing industries.
