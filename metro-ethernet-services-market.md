<p><p>Metro Ethernet Services encompass various market types, including E-Line, E-Access, E-Transit, and others. E-Line provides point-to-point Ethernet connectivity, allowing businesses to connect multiple sites directly. E-Access focuses on delivering Ethernet services to end users through a local loop. E-Transit offers a way to connect different Ethernet networks over a larger area, functioning as a bridge. Other services may include managed Ethernet solutions and Ethernet Virtual Private Networks (EVPN), catering to diverse business needs and enhancing network efficiency.</p></p>
