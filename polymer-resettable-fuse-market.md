<p><p>The Polymer Resettable Fuse market features two primary types: In-Line and Surface Mount. In-Line polymer resettable fuses are designed to be integrated into circuits as discrete components, providing protection against overcurrent conditions in a straightforward manner. Surface Mount polymer resettable fuses, on the other hand, are compact and suitable for automated assembly processes, making them ideal for high-density applications. Both types enhance circuit safety by resetting automatically after the fault is cleared, ensuring continued performance without the need for replacement.</p></p>
