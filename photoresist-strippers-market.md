<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresist Stripper</li><li>Negative Photoresist Stripper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photoresist strippers market is primarily segmented into positive and negative photoresist strippers. Positive photoresist strippers are designed to remove photoresist materials that become soluble after exposure to light, allowing for precise pattern creation in semiconductor manufacturing. Conversely, negative photoresist strippers target materials that polymerize upon exposure, making them resistant to the stripper solutions. Both types are crucial in the semiconductor and microelectronics sectors, aiding in the fabrication process by ensuring clean substrate surfaces for subsequent applications.</p></p>
