<p><p>The turbidity analyzer market is categorized into dry media and wet media types based on the measurement methods used. Dry media turbidity analyzers utilize powders or reagents that are mixed with water samples to assess clarity, often providing cost-effective and portable solutions. In contrast, wet media analyzers measure turbidity directly in the liquid sample, employing sensors and optical technologies for real-time monitoring. Both types are essential for water quality management across various applications, including environmental monitoring and industrial processes.</p></p>
