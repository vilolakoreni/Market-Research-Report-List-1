<p><p>The Industrial Calcium Sulfate market is categorized mainly into two types: Powder and Lump. Powdered calcium sulfate is finely crushed and widely used in various industries for its quick solubility and ease of use in applications like cement production and as a desiccant. Lump calcium sulfate, on the other hand, appears in larger chunks and is typically favored for applications requiring slow dissolution or structural integrity, such as in construction materials and soil conditioning. Each form serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
