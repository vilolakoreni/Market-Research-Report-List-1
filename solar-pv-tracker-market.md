<p><ul><li>Single Axis Tracker</li><li>Dual Axis Tracker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Solar PV Tracker market consists of two primary types: single-axis and dual-axis trackers. Single-axis trackers rotate on one axis, tilting solar panels to follow the sun’s path throughout the day, optimizing energy capture. They are cost-effective and widely used in large solar farms. Dual-axis trackers, on the other hand, adjust on both vertical and horizontal axes, allowing for maximum sunlight exposure year-round. Though more expensive, they significantly increase energy output, making them suitable for high-efficiency applications.</p></p>
