<p><strong>The 3D X-ray Microscope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transmission X-Ray Microscope</li><li>Scanning X-Ray Microscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D X-ray Microscope market primarily consists of Transmission X-ray Microscopes and Scanning X-ray Microscopes. Transmission X-ray Microscopes utilize a transmission technique, where X-rays pass through a sample, providing high-resolution images of its internal structure. In contrast, Scanning X-ray Microscopes employ a scanning approach, focusing X-rays on specific areas to create detailed 3D images, often revealing surface features and chemical composition. Both types are essential for advanced materials research, biological studies, and semiconductor analysis.</p></p>
