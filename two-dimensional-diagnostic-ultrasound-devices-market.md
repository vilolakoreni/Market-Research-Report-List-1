<p><strong>The Two-dimensional Diagnostic Ultrasound Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fanshaped Scanning</li><li>Linear Scanning</li><li>Convex Array Scanning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Two-dimensional diagnostic ultrasound devices incorporate various scanning techniques to visualize internal structures. Fanshaped scanning utilizes a piezoelectric transducer to create a wide, fan-like beam, ideal for viewing large areas such as the heart. Linear scanning emits parallel waves, providing high-resolution images of superficial structures, commonly used in vascular studies. Convex array scanning combines elements of both, producing a curvilinear image that balances depth penetration and field coverage, often employed in abdominal imaging. Each method serves distinct diagnostic needs.</p></p>
