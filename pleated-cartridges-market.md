<p><p>The pleated cartridges market includes various filter types, each serving specific applications. PP (polypropylene) cartridges are known for their chemical resistance and are commonly used for water filtration. PES (polyethersulfone) cartridges offer high flow rates and thermal stability, ideal for pharmaceutical and biotechnology processes. PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) cartridges provide excellent chemical compatibility, making them suitable for aggressive solvents. Nylon cartridges are favored for their broad chemical compatibility and are often used in laboratory applications, enhancing filtration efficiency across industries.</p></p>
