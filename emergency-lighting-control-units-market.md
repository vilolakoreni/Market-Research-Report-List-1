<p><p>Emergency Lighting Control Units (ELCUs) are essential for ensuring safety during power outages, with two primary market types: wired and wireless. Wired ELCUs involve direct connections to the building's electrical system, offering reliable performance and often lower initial maintenance costs, but can be complex to install. Each type caters to specific needs based on installation complexity and building requirements.</p></p>
