<p><p>The PCB laser drilling system market offers various technologies, primarily CO2 and UV laser drilling systems. CO2 laser drilling systems utilize infrared laser beams, suitable for thicker materials and conventional drilling applications, providing high-speed and precision. In contrast, UV laser drilling systems employ shorter wavelengths, allowing for fine feature drilling on advanced materials, making them ideal for high-density interconnections. Both systems cater to the evolving demands of the electronics industry, emphasizing precision and speed in PCB fabrication processes.</p></p>
