<p><p>The high-purity lithium metal market is primarily categorized into two types: salt lake brine and lithium ore. Salt lake brine extraction involves obtaining lithium from naturally occurring brine pools, which are rich in lithium salts. This method is often considered more sustainable and cost-effective. Conversely, lithium ore involves mining hard rock lithium deposits, such as spodumene, which requires more intensive processing. Both sources play crucial roles in meeting the increasing demand for high-purity lithium for batteries and other advanced applications.</p></p>
