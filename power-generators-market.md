<p><p>The power generators market consists mainly of two types: portable and standby generators. Portable generators are designed for temporary use, providing electricity for camping, outdoor events, or during power outages. They are lightweight and easily transportable. In contrast, standby generators are permanently installed and automatically activate during power failures, supplying continuous power to homes or businesses. Both types cater to different needs, with portable generators focusing on mobility and convenience, while standby generators ensure reliability and uninterrupted power supply.</p></p>
