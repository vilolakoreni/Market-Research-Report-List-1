<p><ul><li>"Vacuum"</li><li>"Non-vacuum"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mobile Drying Cabinet market is segmented into two main types: vacuum and non-vacuum. Vacuum drying cabinets operate by creating a low-pressure environment, which accelerates moisture evaporation, making them suitable for sensitive materials that require gentle drying. Non-vacuum cabinets use traditional convection methods, relying on heat circulation to remove moisture, making them ideal for more durable items. Both types cater to different applications, from industrial uses to laboratory settings, enhancing efficiency in drying processes.</p></p>
