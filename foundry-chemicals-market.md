<p><p>The **Foundry Chemicals Market** encompasses various types of materials essential for metal casting processes. **Binders** are used to hold mold materials together, ensuring structural integrity. **Additive Agents** enhance properties like strength, permeability, or thermal stability. **Coatings** are applied to molds to improve surface finish and prevent defects. **Fluxes** remove impurities during melting, ensuring cleaner metal. **Hot Topping Compounds** reduce shrinkage and defects in castings. **Others** include specialized chemicals for unique casting needs, such as refractories or release agents, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
