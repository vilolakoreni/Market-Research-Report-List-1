<p><p>The insulating oil market comprises three main types: Mineral Oil-based Insulating Oil, Silicone-based Insulating Oil, and Synthetic Oil-based Insulating Oil. Silicone-based oil offers enhanced thermal stability and environmental safety, making it ideal for high-performance applications. Each type addresses specific needs within electrical insulation and cooling systems.</p></p>
