<p><p>The organic coconut oil market comprises two main types: virgin and refined. Virgin organic coconut oil is extracted from fresh coconut meat without chemical processes, preserving its natural flavor, aroma, and nutrients, making it ideal for culinary uses and skincare. Refined organic coconut oil, on the other hand, is produced from dried copra and undergoes processing to remove impurities, resulting in a neutral flavor and higher smoke point, suitable for high-heat cooking and industrial applications. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
