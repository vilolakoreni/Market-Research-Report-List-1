<p><ul><li>Concave</li><li>Convex</li><li>Concave-convex</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The telescope lens market includes three primary types: concave, convex, and concave-convex lenses. Concave lenses diverge light rays, creating a virtual image, commonly used in various optical devices. Convex lenses converge light rays to form real images, essential for telescopes and cameras. Concave-convex lenses, combining both shapes, offer versatile optical properties, enabling adjustments in focus and image quality. Each type serves specific purposes in enhancing visual clarity and magnification capabilities in astronomical and terrestrial observations.</p></p>
