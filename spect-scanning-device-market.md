<p><p>SPECT scanning devices utilize various radiopharmaceuticals to enhance imaging capabilities. Iodine-123 and Technetium-99m are widely used for cardiac and thyroid imaging due to their favorable properties. Xenon-133 is effective for pulmonary imaging, assessing lung function. Thallium-201 is often employed for myocardial perfusion studies, particularly in cardiac stress tests. Fluorine-18, although primarily used in PET scans, is increasingly integrated into SPECT applications for its high sensitivity and specificity in detecting tumors and other abnormalities.</p></p>
