<p><strong>The Plastic Mould Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Compression Moulding Moulds</li><li>Extrusion Moulding Moulds</li><li>Injection Moulding Moulds</li><li>Blow Moulding Moulds</li><li>Foam Moulding Moulds</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic mould market encompasses several key types: </p><p>1. **Compression Moulding Moulds**: Used for shaping materials under heat and pressure, ideal for thermosetting plastics.</p><p>2. **Extrusion Moulding Moulds**: Create continuous profiles by forcing molten plastic through a die, suitable for tubes and sheets.</p><p>3. **Injection Moulding Moulds**: Inject molten plastic into a mould cavity, enabling high-volume production of intricate shapes.</p><p>4. **Blow Moulding Moulds**: Form hollow objects by inflating molten plastic, commonly used for bottles.</p><p>5. **Foam Moulding Moulds**: Produce lightweight, insulated products by expanding plastic foam, often seen in packaging and construction materials.</p></p>
