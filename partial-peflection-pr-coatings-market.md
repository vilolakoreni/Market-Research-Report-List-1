<p><p>The Partial Reflection (PR) Coatings market is divided into two primary types: Single Wavelength PR Coatings and Broadband Wavelength PR Coatings. Single Wavelength PR Coatings are designed to reflect a specific wavelength of light while transmitting others, making them suitable for precise applications like laser optics. In contrast, Broadband Wavelength PR Coatings reflect a wider range of wavelengths, catering to applications that require versatility across multiple frequencies, such as in telecommunications and imaging systems. Both types enhance optical performance in various industries.</p></p>
