<p><p>The palm butter market primarily comprises two main product types: crude palm oil and palm olein. Crude palm oil is the unrefined oil extracted from the fruit of the oil palm, possessing high levels of saturated fats and used in food and industrial applications. Both products serve diverse industries, impacting global supply and pricing dynamics.</p></p>
