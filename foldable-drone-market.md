<p><p>The foldable drone market is primarily categorized into two motor types: brushed and brushless motors. Brushed motors are simpler, typically more affordable, and easier to maintain, making them suitable for entry-level drones. However, they tend to have shorter lifespans and less efficiency. In contrast, brushless motors offer higher performance, greater efficiency, and longer durability, making them ideal for advanced models. As the market evolves, the preference for brushless motors is increasing due to their enhanced capabilities and operational life.</p></p>
