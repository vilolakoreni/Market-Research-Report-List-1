<p><p>The motorboat market consists of three main types: outboard motorboats, sterndrive motorboats, and inboard motorboats. Outboard motorboats have engines mounted externally, offering high maneuverability and easier maintenance, making them suitable for various water conditions. Sterndrive motorboats combine an inboard engine with an outboard drive unit, balancing power and efficiency while providing good speed and handling. Inboard motorboats feature engines mounted inside the hull, known for stability and spaciousness, often preferred for larger vessels used in leisure and commercial activities.</p></p>
