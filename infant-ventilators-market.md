<p><p>The infant ventilators market includes several types tailored to support neonatal respiratory care. Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) delivers a steady airflow to keep airways open, aiding newborns with breathing difficulties. Noninvasive Positive Pressure Ventilation (NIPPV) offers assistance by delivering pressurized air through a mask, facilitating easier breathing without invasive procedures. Additionally, the "Other" segment encompasses various emerging technologies and systems designed to meet specific needs in neonatal care, including high-frequency ventilation and advanced monitoring systems.</p></p>
