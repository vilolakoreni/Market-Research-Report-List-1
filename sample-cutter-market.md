<p><p>The sample cutter market can be segmented into three main types: manual, pneumatic, and electric. Pneumatic sample cutters use compressed air for cutting, providing more power and efficiency, ideal for medium to larger operations. Electric sample cutters are powered by electricity, offering high precision and speed, making them well-suited for high-volume applications in various industries such as textiles, food processing, and construction.</p></p>
