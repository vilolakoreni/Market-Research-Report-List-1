<p><p>The heat exchanger market comprises various types, each serving specific applications. Shell and tube heat exchangers are widely used for their durability and efficiency, featuring a series of tubes where heat transfer occurs. Plate heat exchangers provide a compact design, ideal for applications with limited space. Fin type heat exchangers enhance heat transfer efficiency by increasing surface area, suitable for air handling. Air cooled heat exchangers utilize ambient air for cooling, making them energy-efficient in systems where water is scarce.</p></p>
