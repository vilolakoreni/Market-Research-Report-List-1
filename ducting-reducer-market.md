<p><strong>The Ducting Reducer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Concentric Reducer</li><li>Eccentric Reducer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ducting reducer market encompasses two primary types: concentric and eccentric reducers. Concentric reducers feature a symmetrical design that maintains a consistent axis, facilitating smooth fluid flow and is mostly used in vertical applications. Conversely, eccentric reducers are designed with an offset axis, allowing for better drainage of fluids and optimal flow rates in horizontal installations. Both types are essential in HVAC systems, industrial applications, and piping networks, enabling efficient transitions between different duct sizes while minimizing turbulence and pressure loss.</p></p>
